Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun. Mars' surface is orange-red because to the presence of iron(III) oxide dust, earning it the nickname "the Red Planet".
Mars is one of the brightest objects in the sky, and its high-contrast albedo has made it a popular target for telescope viewing.
